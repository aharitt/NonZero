# NonZero — User Manual

## What is NonZero?

NonZero helps you build consistent habits by making every day count. The core idea is simple: a **Non-Zero Day** is any day where you made at least some progress on a habit — even a little. Log your tasks daily, build streaks, and track your resilience over time.

---

## The Four Tabs

### Today
Your daily dashboard. Each task shows your current value for the day and quick-action buttons to log progress.

- **Boolean tasks** — tap **Mark Done** to mark it complete. Shows "Did It!" once logged.
- **Count tasks** — tap **+1 / +5 / +10** to add counts, or tap the pencil icon to enter a custom value.
- **Time tasks** — tap **+5m / +15m / +30m** to add minutes manually, or tap ▶ to start the built-in timer. Tap **Stop** to save the elapsed time.
- **Pencil icon** — opens a detail editor where you can set any value and add an optional note.
- The title bar turns green and reads **"Today is Non-Zero"** once enough tasks are completed.
- Pull down to sync HealthKit data, or tap the sync button (↺♥) in the title bar.

### Tasks
Manage your habit list.

- Tap **+** to add a new task. Choose a name, type, minimum value, and optional goal.
- **Long-press** a task (hold 0.5 seconds) to reveal the **Edit** and **Delete** buttons.
- Tap **Edit** to change the name, type, minimum, goal, and integrations.
- Tap **Reorder** to drag tasks into a custom order.

**Task types:**
| Type | Non-Zero when… |
|---|---|
| Boolean | Marked as done (value ≥ 1) |
| Count | Logged count ≥ minimum |
| Time | Logged minutes ≥ minimum |

### Stats
Analytics for all your tasks.

- **Day Score card** (pinned at top) — your overall daily performance across all tasks. Tap to see detail: Non-Zero Days, Comeback count, Resilience Index, streaks, and completion rates.
- **Per-task cards** — quick stats (Comeback, Resilience, Streak) for each task. Tap any card for the full detail view.
- **Task Detail view** includes:
  - Non-Zero Days, Comeback count, Resilience Index, Days to Return, Current & Best Streak
  - Completion rates for the last 7 / 30 / 90 days
  - **60-day heatmap** — long-press any cell to edit that day's entry. All stats update immediately after saving.
  - Recent Entries list (last 10 non-zero entries)
- If you have many tasks, swipe left/right (or use the arrows) to page through them.

## Key Concepts

**Non-Zero Day** — A day where your logged value meets or exceeds the task's minimum. Even one push-up counts.

**Streak** — Consecutive Non-Zero days. The streak continues even if you log retroactively from the heatmap.

**Day Score** — A daily score across all tasks. You set the threshold (e.g. 60% means 3 out of 5 tasks must be Non-Zero).

**Comeback** — Returning to a Non-Zero day after one or more missed days.

**Resilience Index** — A percentage that measures how quickly and consistently you bounce back after missed days. Recent comebacks are weighted more heavily than older ones (half-life: 30 days). A score near 100% means you return fast; lower scores mean slow or infrequent recovery.

## Tips

- **Edit past entries** — go to Stats → tap a task → long-press any day on the heatmap.
- **Change task type** — long-press a task in the Tasks tab → Edit → change the Type picker. Existing entry values are kept as-is.
- **Archive vs. Delete** — archiving hides a task from Today but preserves all history. Delete removes everything permanently.
- **Backup your data** — Settings → Manage Data → Export Full Backup before updating or switching devices.
